Procedures for Thinking and Behaving Ethically in Workplace

Provide procedures to help engineers to think and behave ethically in the workplace as well as to complain about any ethical issue.

Find Out More

Background

As employees during work, we found that there are two key points that stop us from being ethical, which are: the awareness of being ethical and trusted complaining flow. One can't perform ethics if he or she doesn't understand what the ethics are; The ethical environment can't be set up without complete complaint flow to eliminate people's various concern. These are the main reasons we have this procedure to facilitate the application of being ethical.

Get Started!

Four Steps

Periodically Learning

Companies should provide periodic code of conduct courses to help to build employees' awareness for being ethical.

Ethical Dilemma Cases

Engineers should always work on identifying various ethical dilemma cases during work.

Ethical Complain Procedure

Independent 3rd party ethical review committee should be provided and employee can choose to be anonymous according to their willingness.

Escalation

Escalation channels should be provided in case of employee have concerns about local departments.
